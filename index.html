Research Focus

About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the ACTION research team (Therapeutic ACTion guided by multimodality Imaging in ONcology) specializing in medical image analysis, AI, and computer vision. My work focuses on the therapeutic monitoring of colorectal cancer liver metastases using artificial intelligence techniques.
